Process

Simple sample flow, MOQ, and lead time.

Buyers need to know how to start. This page gives them a simple path from first inquiry to sample, trial order, and repeat supply.

Commercial starting point

Sample-first, then a small MOQ trial, then repeatable production once the buyer is satisfied.

1. Browse
A buyer sees the product line, the trust message, and the buyer kit before deciding whether to enquire.
2. Sample path
We share a sample price and confirm finish preferences, size, and the best design family for the buyer's use case.
3. Trial MOQ
Typical launch MOQ starts at 5 to 10 pieces, depending on design and finish requirements.
4. Lead time
First production runs are planned around 10 to 21 days, with packaging and dispatch included in the workflow.
5. Repeat order
Once finish and packing are approved, the buyer can repeat the order or request private-label variants.
