FAQ

A good website removes friction before the first call. These answers are written for procurement teams, interior buyers, retail partners, and hospitality buyers.

Short answers

Designed to reduce back-and-forth and help a buyer move quickly to a sample request.

What do you make?

Premium geometric metal wall art and powder-coated decor for domestic B2B buyers.

Are you a real manufacturer?

Yes. Metallique Handicraft is the modern brand front for a Moradabad manufacturing base with four decades of strength behind it.

Can you do small quantities first?

Yes. The launch model is sample-first and trial-friendly, with a small MOQ path before repeat supply.

What is the usual lead time?

First production runs are planned around 10 to 21 days depending on design, finish, and quantity.

Can you support private-label work?

Yes. The product line is built to support repeatable finishes and buyer-specific variants once the first order is approved.

How do I start?

Open the product page, review the buyer kit, then use the contact page to request a sample or trial quote.
